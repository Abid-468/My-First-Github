One Piece

One Piece follows Monkey D. Luffy, a rubber-bodied pirate who ate a Devil Fruit, as he gathers a diverse crew (the Straw Hat Pirates) to sail the dangerous Grand Line in search of the ultimate treasure, the "One Piece," left by the late Pirate King Gol D. Roger, all to become the next Pirate King and pursue dreams, friendship, and adventure. The plot centers on epic voyages, battles against powerful foes like the World Government and rival pirates, exploring unique islands, and uncovering the world's hidden history.

  • Monkey D. Luffy: Captain.
  • Roronoa Zoro: Swordsman & unofficial First Mate.
  • Nami: Navigator & Quartermaster.
  • Usopp: Sniper.
  • Sanji: Cook.
  • Tony Tony Chopper: Doctor.
  • Nico Robin: Archaeologist & Historian.
  • Franky: Shipwright.
  • Brook: Musician.
  • Jinbe: Helmsman (Steersman)

Goal of Every crew members:

  • Monkey D. Luffy (Captain): To find the One Piece treasure and become the King of the Pirates.
  • Roronoa Zoro (Swordsman): To become the world's greatest swordsman.
  • Nami (Navigator): To draw a map of the entire world.
  • Usopp (Sniper): To become a brave warrior of the sea.
  • Vinsmoke Sanji (Cook): To find the mythical All Blue sea.
  • Tony Tony Chopper (Doctor): To become a doctor who can cure any disease.
  • Nico Robin (Archaeologist): To uncover the true history of the world by reading Poneglyphs.
  • Franky (Shipwright): To build a ship that can travel the world.
  • Brook (Musician): To reunite with his whale friend, Laboon, at Reverse Mountain.
  • Jinbe (Helmsman): To achieve equality between Fish-Men and humans.
